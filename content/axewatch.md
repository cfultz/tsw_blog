# AxeWatch: Fleet Command for Bitaxe

**AxeWatch** is a professional-grade hardware monitoring utility built specifically for Bitaxe Bitcoin miners. Designed with a strict privacy-first architecture, AxeWatch gives you complete control and visibility over your fleet without ever compromising your data.

### Core Features

* **Real-Time LAN Monitoring:** Instantly track your hashrate, temperatures, and pool settings directly over your local network.
* **Thermal & Hardware Alerts:** Custom background monitoring and vibration patterns instantly alert you to critical overheating events.
* **Zombie Node Detection:** Get notified immediately if a miner stops submitting shares to the pool.
* **Hardware Tuning Profiles:** Create, save, and apply custom voltage and frequency profiles across your entire fleet.
* **Block Celebration:** Catch the magic moment with custom UI popups when your node finds a block.
* **Multi-Language Support:** Fully localized in English, Spanish, German, Portuguese, Japanese, and Chinese.

### Absolute Privacy
AxeWatch operates entirely on your local network. Your miner data, hashrate, and pool configurations are never transmitted to our servers or any third-party analytics trackers. There are no accounts, no cloud backends, and no tracking pixels.
