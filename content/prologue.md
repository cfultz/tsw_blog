# Prologue: Your Library, Your Rules

**Prologue** is a sleek, modern mobile client built specifically for [Audiobookshelf](https://www.audiobookshelf.org/). It bridges the gap between your self-hosted audiobook collection and your mobile device, providing a premium listening experience without the premium data mining.

### Core Features

* **Direct Server Connection:** Log in securely using your own server's URL. Prologue acts as a direct bridge to your Audiobookshelf instance.
* **Offline Playback:** Download your favorite audiobooks directly to your device for listening on the go, without eating up your cellular data.
* **Progress Syncing:** Seamlessly sync your playback progress back to your self-hosted server so you never lose your place.
* **Local Caching:** Album art and metadata are cached locally on your device for a fast, snappy UI experience.

### Zero Telemetry
Prologue is a "pass-through" tool. We do not operate a middleman server, we do not intercept your login credentials, and we do not track what you listen to. Your data travels securely and directly from your device to your server.
