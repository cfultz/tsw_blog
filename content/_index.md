Welcome to **Two Secret Weapons**, a development studio dedicated to building privacy-first tools. We believe your data belongs to you—no tracking, no telemetry, no cloud accounts.

### Our Applications

* **[AxeWatch](/axewatch/)** Professional real-time monitoring, thermal alerts, and fleet management for your Bitaxe hardware. Your miner data never leaves your local network.

* **[Prologue](/prologue/)** A beautiful, pass-through client for your self-hosted Audiobookshelf instance. Stream and download your audio library directly from your own server.

* **[RetroStats](https://github.com/cfultz/retrostats-android)** A sleek, open-source Android client for RetroAchievements. Authenticate locally with your API key and track your retro gaming progress directly from the official source.
