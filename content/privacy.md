# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 26, 2026**

Two Secret Weapons ("we," "us," or "our") operates the mobile applications **AxeWatch** and **Prologue** (collectively, the "Services"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Services.

---

### 1. Our Philosophy
We believe that your data belongs to you. Our applications are designed to be "pass-through" tools. We do not operate a cloud backend, we do not use tracking pixels, and we do not monetize your personal information. If we ever decide to change our "zero-data" stance (spoiler: we won't), we will update this page and provide a notification within the apps.

### 2. Data Collection and Usage
We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers. Our applications function entirely on your local device and communicate exclusively with your own hardware or self-hosted servers.

* **AxeWatch (Local Device Data):** The app communicates directly with your Bitaxe mining hardware via your local area network (LAN). Data such as hashrate, temperature, and pool settings are fetched from your hardware and displayed within the app. Your miner data is never transmitted to us or any third-party servers.
* **Prologue (Self-Hosted Data):** Any data processed by the app (such as library metadata, playback progress, or login credentials) is stored locally on your device or sent directly to your self-hosted Audiobookshelf instance. There are no intermediaries.
* **No Personal Information:** We do not require account creation on our end, email registration, or any personal identifying information (PII) to use our Services.

### 3. Permissions and Background Access
To provide the core functionality of our apps, specific device permissions are required:

* **Local Network Access (Both):** Required to discover and communicate with Bitaxe miners on your Wi-Fi/LAN, or to connect to your local Audiobookshelf server.
* **Storage Access (Prologue):** Used to download audiobooks for offline playback and to cache album art and metadata locally.
* **Foreground Service / DATA_SYNC (AxeWatch):** Used to maintain a stable connection with your hardware for real-time monitoring. This allows the app to provide critical thermal alerts and "Zombie Node" notifications even when the app is in the background.
* **Notifications (Both):** Used to alert you of hardware issues (AxeWatch) or to display media playback controls (Prologue).

### 4. Third-Party Services
We use standard platform services for app distribution and monetization:

* **Google Play Store / Apple App Store:** These platforms manage app installation and In-App Purchases (IAP). They may collect data as governed by their respective privacy policies.
* **No Analytics or Tracking:** We do not use third-party telemetry, advertising IDs, crash reporting services, or analytics frameworks (such as Firebase Analytics or Mixpanel) that phone home to us.

### 5. Data Security
Because your data remains on your physical device, local network, or self-hosted server, the security of your information relies primarily on your own infrastructure. We recommend using strong Wi-Fi passwords, keeping your Bitaxe firmware (AxeOS) updated, and ensuring proper SSL/TLS encryption on your Audiobookshelf server.

### 6. Children's Privacy
Our Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

### 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the top.
