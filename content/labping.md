# LabPing: Homelab Monitoring, Localized.

**LabPing** is the ultimate local-first monitoring dashboard built specifically for homelabbers, self-hosters, and sysadmins. Designed from the ground up to respect your local network, LabPing natively handles self-signed SSL certificates and local IP addresses—giving you complete visibility over your infrastructure without a single cloud account.

### Core Features

* **Real-Time Monitoring:** Track the uptime, latency, and status of all your HTTP/HTTPS and TCP services.
* **Background Alerts:** Enable the persistent background service to keep a live count of your online nodes, and receive instant push notifications if a service drops past your failure threshold.
* **Detailed Analytics:** Dive into individual nodes to view total uptime percentages, recent ping history grids, and beautiful latency heartbeat line charts.
* **Smart Favicons:** Automatically pulls your service's favicon, gracefully falling back to a clean, themed letter avatar if an icon isn't found.
* **Total Data Control:** Easily export and import your node configurations as a local JSON backup.
* **Premium Themes:** Customize your dashboard with stunning, developer-focused color palettes including Slate, Cyberpunk, Nord, and Dracula.

### Absolute Privacy
LabPing operates entirely on your device and your local network. Your node configurations, IP addresses, and ping history are stored exclusively on your phone. We do not track what services you run, and there is zero third-party telemetry.
